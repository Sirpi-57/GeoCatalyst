Test Instructions

General Instructions:

  1. Total duration of this test is -- minutes.
  2. The clock will be set on the server. The countdown timer at the top right corner of screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the examination. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will end by itself. You will not be required to end or submit your examination.
  3. The Question Palette displayed on the right side of screen will show the status of each question using one of the following symbols:
    • 1 You have not visited the question yet.
    • 2 You have not answered the question.
    • 3 You have answered the question.
    • 4 You have NOT answered the question, but have marked the question for review.
    • 5 You have answered the question, but marked it for review.
    The Marked for Review status for a question simply indicates that you would like to look at that question again. If a question is answered and Marked for Review, your answer for that question will be considered in the evaluation.
  4. A scientific calculator is available at the top-right hand side of the screen.

Navigating Questions:

  1. To navigate to a question, click on the question number in the Question Palette.
  2. Click on 'Save & Next' to save your answer for the current question and then go to the next question.
  3. Click on 'Mark for Review & Next' to mark the current question for review, save your answer (if selected), and then go to the next question.
  4. Caution: Note that your answer for the current question will not be saved if you navigate to another question directly clicking on a question number without saving the answer to the previous question.

Answering Questions:

  1. For multiple choice type questions (MCQ): Select your answer by clicking on the bubble button beside the option. To deselect, click the bubble button again or click 'Clear Response'.
  2. For multiple select type questions (MSQ): Select your answer(s) by clicking on the checkbox(es) beside the options. To deselect, click the checkbox again or click 'Clear Response'.
  3. For numerical answer type questions (NAT): Enter your answer using the virtual numeric keypad.
  4. To change your answer, choose another option (MCQ/MSQ) or edit the numerical value (NAT).
  5. To save your answer, you MUST click on 'Save & Next'.
  6. To mark a question for review, click on 'Mark for Review & Next'. If an answer is selected for a question that is Marked for Review, that answer will be considered in the evaluation.
